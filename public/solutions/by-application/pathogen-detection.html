Comprehensive Pathogen Coverage

Validated assays for the most critical foodborne pathogens

Salmonella

All serotypes including S. Enteritidis, S. Typhimurium, and emerging strains

  • Time to result: 18-24 hours
  • Sensitivity: 1-5 CFU/25g
  • Matrices: Meat, poultry, dairy, produce, environmental

Listeria

Listeria monocytogenes and Listeria spp. screening

  • Time to result: 24-30 hours (L. mono), 24 hours (spp.)
  • Sensitivity: 1-5 CFU/25g
  • Matrices: RTE foods, dairy, seafood, environmental

E. coli O157:H7 & STEC

O157:H7 and the "Big 6" non-O157 STEC serotypes

  • Time to result: 10-24 hours
  • Sensitivity: 1 CFU/25g
  • Matrices: Beef, produce, flour, dairy

Campylobacter

C. jejuni, C. coli, and C. lari detection

  • Time to result: 24-48 hours
  • Sensitivity: 1-10 CFU/25g
  • Matrices: Poultry, raw milk, water

Cronobacter

Cronobacter sakazakii for infant formula and powdered products

  • Time to result: 24 hours
  • Sensitivity: 1 CFU/10g
  • Matrices: Powdered infant formula, dairy

Vibrio

V. parahaemolyticus, V. vulnificus, V. cholerae

  • Time to result: 8-24 hours
  • Sensitivity: 10-100 CFU/g
  • Matrices: Shellfish, seafood, water
