About FastPath Diagnostics

Advancing food safety through rapid, accurate microbiology testing

Our Mission

Speed matters in quality control. Every hour a batch sits in hold represents cost, complexity, and risk.

Traditional microbiological testing methods require 48-72 hours for results. During this time, products remain in inventory limbo—consuming warehouse space, tying up working capital, and creating logistical bottlenecks that ripple through the supply chain.

FastPath Diagnostics was founded on a straightforward premise: food safety testing should deliver results at the speed of modern production, not at the pace of century-old culture methods.

Our mission is to provide food and beverage manufacturers with testing solutions that enable same-day batch release decisions without compromising accuracy or regulatory acceptance.

Quality & Validation Approach

Built for Regulatory Acceptance

Our quality management system is designed around ISO 17025 principles and the requirements of European food safety regulatory frameworks. Every FastPath method undergoes rigorous internal validation before external evaluation.

Validation Framework

  • Inclusivity/exclusivity testing against comprehensive strain panels
  • Matrix-specific performance characterisation
  • Ruggedness studies across operators and environmental conditions
  • Comparative studies against EN ISO reference methods
  • Independent third-party validation studies

Regulatory Alignment (EU/UK)

FastPath methods are designed for alignment with EN ISO 16140 reference methods and European food safety frameworks including Regulation (EC) 2073/2005 on microbiological criteria for foodstuffs. We pursue MicroVal and NordVal certification for applicable assays.

Specific validation status and regulatory certifications vary by method and matrix. Contact us for current validation documentation.
