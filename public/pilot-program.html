Eligibility Requirements

The pilot program is designed for food manufacturers ready to evaluate rapid pathogen detection.

  • FDA-regulated food manufacturing facility (dairy, meat, poultry, produce, beverages, RTE, etc.)
  • Minimum 10 pathogen tests per week (current or planned testing volume)
  • Dedicated space for system installation (approx. 3ft x 3ft benchtop)
  • Assigned staff member to participate in training and evaluation
  • Decision-maker engagement for end-of-pilot evaluation
