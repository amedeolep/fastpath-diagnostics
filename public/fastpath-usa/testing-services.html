Sample Requirements

Simple collection protocols aligned with industry standards

Sample Size

Standard 25g analytical unit per FDA BAM guidelines. Larger samples may be required for specific matrices - detailed guidance provided with sample collection kits.

Collection Kits

Pre-configured sample collection kits provided for Express Service customers. Kits include sterile containers, temperature indicators, and shipping materials.

Enrichment Protocols

Compatible with standard FDA BAM and USDA MLG enrichment protocols. Pre-enrichment can be performed on-site before sample submission or system processing.
