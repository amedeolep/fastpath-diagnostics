The FastPath Platform

A fully integrated workflow from sample to result

1. Sample Input & Preparation

Our proprietary sample preparation technology processes complex food matrices quickly and efficiently, preparing samples for molecular analysis.

  • Compatible with standard enrichment protocols (FDA BAM, USDA MLG)
  • Automated lysis and purification
  • Advanced debris clearance for complex matrices
  • Viable pathogen isolation and concentration

2. Rapid Molecular Detection

Proprietary molecular biology platform enables detection of target pathogens with exceptional sensitivity and specificity in a fraction of the traditional time.

  • Real-time molecular amplification technology
  • Multi-target detection in single reaction
  • Quantitative CFU determination capabilities
  • Species and strain-level identification

3. Automated Analysis & Reporting

Built-in data analysis eliminates manual interpretation and delivers clear, actionable results with full traceability.

  • Real-time data analysis during detection
  • Automated positive/negative determination
  • Digital reporting with complete audit trail
  • LIMS/QMS integration via secure API
