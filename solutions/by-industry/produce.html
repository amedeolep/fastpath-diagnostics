Produce Industry Challenges

Understanding the unique food safety requirements of fresh produce

Agricultural Water

Irrigation water can be a source of contamination. FSMA requires testing of agricultural water and corrective actions for unsafe results.

Soil Amendments

Biological soil amendments of animal origin (BSAAO) like manure and compost can introduce pathogens if not properly treated.

No Kill Step

Most fresh produce is consumed raw with no cooking step to eliminate pathogens, making prevention and detection critical.

Traceability

Rapid trace-back during outbreaks requires robust documentation of testing, sourcing, and distribution throughout the supply chain.
