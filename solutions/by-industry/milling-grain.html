Milling & Grain Industry Challenges

Understanding the unique food safety requirements of grain processing

Salmonella Persistence

Salmonella can survive for extended periods in low-moisture environments like flour and grain, making it a persistent challenge even in dry processing facilities.

Mycotoxin Contamination

Fungal toxins like aflatoxin, DON, and ochratoxin can form in grain during growth or storage, posing serious health risks and regulatory challenges.

Dust & Cross-Contamination

Fine grain dust can spread contamination throughout facilities. Environmental monitoring is critical to identify and control harborage sites.

Raw Flour Consumption

Consumer behavior has shifted with more people consuming raw dough and batter, increasing the importance of pathogen testing in flour products.
