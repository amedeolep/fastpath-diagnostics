About FastPath Diagnostics

Founded by food safety scientists with a mission to make every food product safer.

Our Story

FastPath Diagnostics was founded in 2019 by a team of food safety scientists and engineers who saw a critical gap in the industry.

Traditional pathogen testing methods were taking 24-72 hours to deliver results, forcing food manufacturers to either hold inventory or ship products before safety was confirmed. This created unnecessary waste, increased costs, and put consumers at risk.

Our founders asked a simple question: What if we could reduce pathogen detection time from days to hours?

After years of research and development, we launched the FastPath Apex¬Æ System‚Äîthe first fully automated pathogen detection platform capable of delivering actionable results in just 4 hours with 99.9% accuracy.

Today, FastPath protects over 500 food production facilities worldwide, having performed more than 2 million tests. But we're just getting started.
